# picoCTF: Scavenger Hunt

Things I learned from this challenge:

1. Try to access those dot files! And other files that might be accessible other
   than the public ones.

2. `robots.txt` - [https://developers.google.com/search/docs/advanced/robots/intro](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/advanced/robots/intro)

3. Apache server manages its server permissions in `.htaccess` file.

It is always good to know what options I have to find vulnerabilities.
